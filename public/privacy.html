Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Last Updated: February 2026 | Version: 3.0

OUR COMMITMENT TO PRIVACY: MPHaven is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We do not sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (securely hashed).
  • Profile Information: Any information you choose to add to your profile.
  • User Content: Notes, journal entries, reflections, or other content you create within the app.
  • Communications: Information you provide when contacting support or customer service.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Local Storage: Disclaimer acknowledgment, timestamp, version, and user agent stored in your browser's local storage.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, time spent (anonymized).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address (may be collected by third-party services).

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Providers: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google.
  • Payment Processors: Stripe and PayPal process payments and may collect billing information. We do not receive or store full payment credentials.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the app
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Remember your disclaimer acknowledgment
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Payment Processing

IMPORTANT: MPHaven does not store, process, or have access to your full payment credentials. All payments are handled by third-party payment processors:

By making a payment, you agree to the terms and privacy policies of these processors. We encourage you to review their policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Disclaimer acknowledgment and related data are stored locally in your browser. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Server Storage: Account information and user content are stored on Supabase servers located in the United States. We implement reasonable security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, to protect your data.

Payment Data: Full payment credentials are not stored by MPHaven. They are processed and stored by Stripe and PayPal, which are PCI-DSS compliant.

Security Practices: We use industry-standard security practices, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Information: Retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time.
  • User Content: Retained until you delete it or delete your account.
  • Local Storage: Remains on your device until cleared.
  • Payment Records: Retained by Stripe/PayPal according to their policies and legal requirements.
  • Backup Copies: May exist for up to 30 days after deletion.

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA 2026)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: Request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell your data.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Information: Limit use of sensitive personal information. We do not use sensitive information beyond what is necessary.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

7.2 Other State Rights

Residents of other states may have similar rights under applicable state privacy laws, including:

  • Virginia (VCDPA)
  • Colorado (CPA)
  • Connecticut (CTDPA)
  • Utah (UCPA)
  • And others as enacted

7.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at: privacy@mphaven.com

We will verify your identity before processing your request. We may need to request additional information to verify your identity. We will respond within 45 days.

8. Children's Privacy

MPHaven is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@mphaven.com. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete it.

9. International Users

MPHaven is based in the United States and is intended for users in the United States. If you access the app from outside the U.S., please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S., where our servers are located. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your information to the U.S.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Local Storage: We use local storage to remember your disclaimer acknowledgment. This is essential for app functionality.

Third-Party Cookies: Our payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) may use cookies necessary for transaction processing. We do not control these cookies.

Do Not Track: Our app does not respond to "Do Not Track" signals at this time.

Cookie Preferences: You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

11. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by applicable law. Notifications will be sent via email or through the app.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or through the app. Your continued use of the app after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
